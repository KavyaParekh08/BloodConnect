Patient Guidelines

Requesting Blood

Patients or their representatives can request blood through the BloodConnect platform. Ensure all required medical information is accurately provided to facilitate a smooth process.

Eligibility

To receive blood, patients must have a confirmed medical need and meet the criteria set by their healthcare provider. BloodConnect is a facilitator, and final decisions rest with medical professionals.

Process

  1. Submit a request with necessary medical details.
  2. BloodConnect matches the request with available donors.
  3. Donors are notified and can accept the request.
  4. Upon acceptance, the donation process is coordinated.
  5. Blood is delivered to the patient's healthcare facility.

Important Notes

  • BloodConnect is not a substitute for emergency medical services. In emergencies, contact your local emergency services immediately.
  • All donations are subject to medical screening and approval.
  • BloodConnect does not guarantee blood availability but strives to connect patients with potential donors efficiently.
